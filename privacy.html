Privacy Policy

Muslim Prayer Nudge  ·  Battuta Labs LLC  ·  Last updated: June 26, 2026

Overview

Muslim Prayer Nudge ("the App") is developed and operated by Battuta Labs LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Prayer preferences and settings — gender, notification preferences, and other onboarding choices you enter. This information is stored locally on your device.
  • Prayer logs — records of prayers you mark as completed, snoozed, or missed. Stored locally on your device.

Information Collected Automatically

Location Data

  • The App requests access to your device's location solely to calculate accurate Islamic prayer times for your geographic area.
  • Location data is used only on-device for prayer time calculation and is not transmitted to our servers or sold to third parties.
  • You may deny location permission; the App will still function using a manually entered location.

Anonymous Usage Data

  • The App uses Firebase (Google) for anonymous authentication. A random anonymous identifier is generated automatically — no name, email, or personal information is associated with it.
  • If you use the Prayer Circle social feature, your prayer status updates are synced to Firebase Firestore under your anonymous identifier.

Purchase Data

  • If you subscribe to premium features, purchases are processed through RevenueCat and the platform's app store (Google Play or Apple App Store). We do not collect or store your payment information directly. RevenueCat may collect anonymized purchase and subscription data.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • Your precise location history or location data outside of prayer time calculation
  • Any data for advertising purposes

How We Use Information

We use collected information only to:

  • Calculate accurate prayer times for your location
  • Deliver prayer reminder notifications at appropriate times
  • Sync your prayer circle status with other circle members (if you join a circle)
  • Process and verify in-app subscription entitlements

Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Firebase (Google) Anonymous authentication, Prayer Circle sync firebase.google.com/support/privacy
RevenueCat Subscription and purchase management revenuecat.com/privacy
Google Play / Apple App Store App distribution and billing See respective platform policies

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other third parties.

Data Storage and Security

  • Personal prayer logs and preferences are stored locally on your device using on-device storage (Hive). We do not have access to this data.
  • Prayer Circle data synced to Firebase is stored on Google's servers and protected under Google's security practices.
  • We implement reasonable technical measures to protect information, but no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Data Retention

  • Local data remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data.
  • Firebase anonymous account data is retained until deleted. You may request deletion by contacting us.
  • RevenueCat retains purchase records per their data retention policy.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete it.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access information associated with your anonymous account
  • Request deletion of your Firebase anonymous account data
  • Opt out of location access via your device settings at any time
  • Disable notifications via your device settings at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

Changes to This Policy
